Bulk Ordering

Save money when you buy in bulk! We offer discounts on bulk orders depending on the product and the total invoice amount. Minimum value to be eligible for discounts is approximately Rs-999. A bulk order can be placed by anyone from anywhere in the city and will be delivered anywhere in the city. Discounts are available on total invoice value excluding shipping charges. Please note: Since we offer volume discounts, we cannot accept returns on bulk orders.
